Admin Panel — How To Use

This guide explains every section of the admin panel. Read through once and you'll know exactly how to manage your portfolio.

01

🏠 Hero Section — Edit Your Name & Tagline

Click Hero Section in the sidebar. You will see 3 sections:

  • Name & Tagline — Type your name in Line 1, Line 2, Line 3. Write your tagline/description. Set your location badge text.
  • Info Card — Edit the card that floats on the right side. Set school/class, your 3 stats, and the motto strip text.
  • Profile Photo — Click the upload box to pick a photo from your phone. It will instantly appear as your avatar.
💡 Every change appears on your portfolio instantly as you type — no save button needed!
02

👤 About Me — Edit Your Description

Click About Me in the sidebar. You will see 4 input fields:

  • Paragraph 1, 2, 3 — Type your story in each paragraph box. The text updates live on your site.
  • Motto Quote — Change your personal motto that appears in the quote box.
💡 Just type in the boxes — your About section updates immediately!
03

⚡ Skills — Add, Edit & Delete Skills

Click Skills in the sidebar.

  • Add New Skill — Click the green ➕ Add New Skill button. A new skill card appears at the bottom.
  • Edit a Skill — Click on any skill's title bar to expand it. Change the icon (emoji), title, and description. Changes apply live.
  • Delete a Skill — Expand the skill and click the red 🗑 Delete Skill button. Confirm to remove it.
💡 Use any emoji as the skill icon — just paste it in the Icon field!
04

🚀 Projects — Manage Your Work

Click Projects in the sidebar.

  • Add Project — Click ➕ Add New Project. Fill in name, description, tech tags (comma separated), and an optional link.
  • Edit Project — Click on a project's title bar to expand it and edit any field.
  • Delete Project — Expand and click the red delete button.
  • Tech Tags — Write tags separated by commas: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
05

📝 Blog Posts — Write & Publish

Click Blog Posts in the sidebar.

  • Write New Post — Click the green ✏️ Write New Post button. The editor opens at the bottom.
  • Emoji — Set a fun emoji for the blog card image.
  • Category — Set a tag like "Technology" or "Cybersecurity".
  • Title & Excerpt — The excerpt is the short text shown on the card.
  • Full Content — Write the full article here.
  • Edit existing — Click ✏️ Edit next to any post.
  • Delete — Click the 🗑 button next to any post.
06

🔗 Social Links — Connect Your Profiles

Click Social Links in the sidebar.

  • Paste your full link in each box. For example: https://youtube.com/@yourchannel
  • WhatsApp — You can paste your number like +8801XXXXXXXXX and it will auto-convert to a WhatsApp link.
  • Leave a box empty to hide that social icon from your site.
  • Icons appear in your Hero section, Contact section, and Footer automatically.
💡 Platforms: YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, GitHub, Telegram
07

📬 Contact Text — Edit Contact Section

Click Contact Text in the sidebar.

  • Contact Intro — The paragraph visitors read in the Contact section.
  • Footer Text — The copyright text at the very bottom of your site.
08

🖼️ Media Upload — Upload Photos

Click Media Upload in the sidebar.

  • Click the upload box and select one or multiple images from your phone.
  • Uploaded images appear in the Library below.
  • Click the red ✕ on any image to delete it.
💡 Profile photo is uploaded separately in Hero Section → Profile Photo!
09

👥 User Manager — Admin Accounts

Click User Manager in the sidebar.

  • Existing Users — You can see all admin accounts. The OWNER account cannot be deleted.
  • Add New User — Type a username and password, then click ➕ Add User. That person can now log in.
  • Remove User — Click the red 🗑 Remove button next to any non-owner account.
⚠️ Keep your username and password safe. Never share them with anyone you don't trust!
10

👁 Preview & Close Admin

  • Click ✕ Close Admin in the top bar to go back to your portfolio and see all changes.
  • All your data is automatically saved in your browser — it will be there even after you close and reopen the page.
  • To open admin again: go to your site URL and add #admin at the end, then press Enter.
  • Or click the tiny invisible dot at the bottom-right corner of your site.
💾 Your data is saved automatically — you never need to press a Save button!
